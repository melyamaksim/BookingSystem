You can choose what you want to book: Hotel, plane ticket, or a train ticket.
The system shoves the available destinations and information
You choose what ticket or hotel you want and the booking saves to your account

You have to create an account with username and password where the booking will save (it could also be random)
The account will show all your bookings and the information about them
Every booking will have a random id
The bookings save to a file associated with your account
You can delete a booking or change it
The credit card information you input saves to your account
All the hotels and destinations are saved in a file that we create
After someone books the ticket the hotel or destination gets removed from the file
